Work and Projects

Time Series Model

Over the course of this past summer, I developed and utilized a time series model through Facebook’s Prophet to forecast future foot traffic trends in retail stores based on past data points.

Anomaly Detection

For my other project this summer, I established an efficient anomaly detection model based on past forecasting errors to explain deviations in foot traffic.

Research Project

Through an independent study, I used machine learning to build and evaluate a model to distinguish between two subreddits, r/Christianity and r/Atheism. Analysis of the model reveals aspects of each community’s distinct characteristics, specifically their temperaments and tendencies.

Infographic Design

For a past school assignment, I created an infographic on Information Overload. In the infographic, I synthesized and presented a compelling argument on the impact of having too much information.
